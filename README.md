# 🎤 SpeakSmart AI

**AI-Powered Public Speaking Assistant for Confidence, Clarity & Communication Excellence**

SpeakSmart AI is an intelligent web-based platform that helps users improve their public speaking skills using Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Speech Analytics, and NLP. It provides real-time feedback on speech delivery, confidence, grammar, eye contact, posture, gestures, nervousness, and overall communication effectiveness.

Designed for students, professionals, job seekers, presenters, and anyone who wants to become a better speaker.

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Many people struggle with:

- Fear of public speaking
- Nervousness during presentations
- Poor eye contact
- Weak body posture
- Grammar mistakes while speaking
- Lack of confidence
- No proper feedback after practice

Traditional speaking practice often lacks **instant personalized guidance**.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

SpeakSmart AI acts like a **virtual communication coach**.

Users simply speak in front of the camera and microphone. The AI analyzes performance and generates smart insights to improve speaking ability.
